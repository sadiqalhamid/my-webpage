Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. this classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optional filled with your choice of cheese, vegetable, or meats.

preparation Time

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preperation: 5 minutes
  • Time: minutes

INGREDIENT

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

INSTRUCTION

  1. Beat the eggs: a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and papper until they are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
  2. The pan: a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. the omelette: the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. fillings(optional): the eggs begin to set the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. let it cook for another minute, slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
